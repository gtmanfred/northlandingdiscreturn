## Program Name

North Landing Disc Return

## Program Description

North Landing Disc Return is a free service that helps disc golfers recover discs lost at North Landing. Players log found discs with photos; disc owners can sign in, claim their discs, add discs to a wishlist, and receive SMS notifications when a matching disc is found or when a pickup event is scheduled. By providing your phone number and using the service, you consent to receive SMS notifications related to your discs and to pickup events.

## Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging rates from your wireless carrier apply to all SMS messages you send and receive in connection with this program. North Landing Disc Return does not charge a fee to participate.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on activity related to your account. You will typically receive a message when one of your wishlisted or claimed discs is logged, when a pickup event relevant to you is scheduled or updated, and in reply to messages you send us. Most participants receive only a small number of messages per month.

## Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message from us. After you send `STOP`, you will receive a confirmation message and will not receive further SMS messages from the program unless you opt back in. To opt back in, contact support or update your profile and confirm your phone number.
