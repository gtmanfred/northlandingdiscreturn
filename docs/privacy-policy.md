# Privacy Policy

**Program:** North Landing Disc Return
**Last updated:** 2026-04-28

This Privacy Policy describes how North Landing Disc Return ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and SMS notification program.

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information directly from you or from services you use to sign in:

- **Account information** — your name and email address, provided by Google when you sign in with Google OAuth.
- **Phone number** — provided by you in your profile, used to send SMS notifications about your discs.
- **Disc claims and wishlist data** — the discs you have claimed as yours and the discs you have added to your wishlist.
- **Disc photos and metadata** — photos and details of found discs that admins upload to the inventory (uploaded by admins, not by general users).
- **Pickup event participation** — which pickup events you have signed up for or attended.
- **SMS interactions** — inbound messages you send to our SMS number (e.g., `STOP`, `HELP`) and the delivery status of messages we send to you, as reported by our SMS provider.
- **Server logs** — standard request logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) generated when you use the site or when our SMS webhook receives a message.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate you and maintain your session.
- Match found discs to their owners and let you manage your claimed discs and wishlist.
- Send you SMS notifications when one of your discs is found, when a pickup event is scheduled, or when you message us asking for help.
- Honor opt-out requests (e.g., when you reply `STOP`).
- Operate, maintain, and debug the service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

## How We Share Your Information

We share information only with the service providers needed to operate the program:

- **Google** — for OAuth sign-in.
- **Twilio** — to deliver SMS messages and receive your replies.
- **Fly.io** — for backend application hosting.
- **Supabase** — for storage of disc photos.
- **GitHub Pages** — for serving the website.

We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information, claimed-disc records, and wishlist for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us at the support address below. SMS opt-out records are retained as long as necessary to honor your opt-out request.

## Your Choices

- **SMS opt-out** — text `STOP` to our SMS number at any time to stop receiving messages. See the Terms and Conditions for details.
- **Account deletion** — contact us to delete your account and personal data.
- **Profile updates** — update your name and phone number from your profile page at any time.

## Children

This service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## Contact
